# Privacy Policy — DocDaWeb

**Last updated:** June 7, 2026

## Overview

DocDaWeb is a browser extension that converts web page content into LLM-friendly formats (Markdown or clean HTML). It operates entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any user data.

## What data does DocDaWeb access?

When you click "Pick Element" or "Convert Entire Page", the extension reads the DOM content of the currently active tab to extract and convert it. This processing happens **entirely locally** in your browser — no data is sent to any server.

## What data does DocDaWeb store?

None. DocDaWeb does not store any browsing history, page content, personal information, or any other data. Converted content is placed on your clipboard and is not retained by the extension.

## What data does DocDaWeb share?

None. DocDaWeb does not transmit any data to external servers, third-party services, or analytics platforms.

## Permissions used

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `activeTab` | Access the current tab's content when the user explicitly triggers a conversion |
| `clipboardWrite` | Copy converted content to the clipboard |
| `scripting` | Inject the content script into tabs opened before the extension was installed |

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published in the extension's repository with a new "Last updated" date.
